About:

Digital media is digitised content that can be passed over the internet or computer networks. This can include text, audio, video and graphics. This could include social media, digital audio (such as MP3), digital books, and many more options. To transmit this digital media and across the internet, you need to encode it all. Encoding is the process of putting a sequence of characters (letters, numbers, punctuation, and symbols), into a specialised format for efficient transmission or storage.

Text Encoding

Definition

Text encoding, otherwise known as character encoding, is the process of representing individual characters using a corresponding encoding system, such as ASCII or Unicode.

It tells the computer how to interpret raw zeros and ones into real characters.
