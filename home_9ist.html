Digital Media and Web Design

Students learned about digital representation of text and images. Conceptual understanding provided the foundation for exploring ways that digital data can be manipulated, compressed, stored, secured, and displayed.

Informed by the W3C accessibility standards on purpose and audience of choice, students work collaboratively to design and develop websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
