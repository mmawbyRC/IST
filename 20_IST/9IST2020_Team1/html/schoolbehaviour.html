Students are expected to be respectful to their peers, their teachers and the campus.

Behaviour towards peers

Students are expected to be kind to their peers. Bullying in any forms is not tolerated. Girls are to be respectful of others personal space and belongings.

Filming others without their permission at school is prohibited.

Behaviour towards teachers

Students are expected to treat teachers with respect and listen to their instructions at all times. When emailing teachers email etiquette is to be followed.

Behaviour towards the campus

Girls are not to litter anywhere in the school and chewing gum is prohibited. Girls are also discouraged from graffiting the desks.

When in the upper levels of the school, girls are to not throw things off the balcony. Girls are also to keep all their belongings with them. At the end of each term lockers are to be cleaned out of food and books.
