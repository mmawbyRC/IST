Compulsory Clothing

  • White Shirt (short or long sleeved)
  • Skirt
  • Blazer
  • Navy Blue Tights
  • Black School Shoes

Optional Clothing

  • Navy Blue Socks (replacement for tights)
  • Maroon Jumper
  • Gold/silver earrings (studs)

P.E Uniform

  • White Shirt
  • Shorts
  • White Socks
  • Sneakers
  • P.E Jacket
  • Tracksuit Pants

Uniform Rules

  • Skirts must be knee length.
  • Blazers must be worn to and from school (not compulsory during school hours).
  • Socks must be visible (sport socks are not permitted).
  • Jumpers must be carried or put away when not being worn.
  • Jumpers must not be worn to and from school (unless a blazer is being worn over the top).
