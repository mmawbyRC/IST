Students of Roseville College are expected to represent the school and upholds its values in public.

Uniform

Students are expected to wear the correct uniform as per the term whenever in school or outside of school. In Summer, all students must wear their panamas to and from school and in Winter, their blazers. If more clarification is needed on what is required in regards to the uniform, please visit our other pages.

Girls are also required to wear either their panamas or blazers (dependant on the uniform) at all times outside of school.

Transport

When on public transport, students must be kind and helpful. If there is someone else who needs a seat, students are expected to stand and offer their seat to the needier other. If students are seen being disrespectful, rude or not adhering to these rules, the school will be notified. Students must always tap on and off with a valid opal card and follow all laws regarding transport use.

Manner

Girls must be quiet, courteous and responsible at all times. Chewing gum is not allowed in uniform - not in public or at school.
