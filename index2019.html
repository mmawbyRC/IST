9 IST 2019

Digital Media and Web Design

Students learned about digital representation of text and images. Conceptual understanding provided the foundation for exploring ways that digital data can be manipulated, compressed, stored, secured, and displayed.

Informed by the W3C accessibility standards on purpose and audience of choice, students worked collaboratively to design and develop websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

10 IST 2019

AI, Modeling, Simulations, Data Science

Students were challenged to do an in-depth study of emerging Artificial Intelligence (AI) applications. Taking on perspectives other than their own, they justified preferred AI futures based on social and ethical impacts.

Students are learning basic Data Science to help understand how AI could work through modelling and simulation. This includes employing analysis and visualisation skills on bigger data sets.

10 IST's work on AI is featured in Digital Technologies (DT) Hub as an exemplar for the teaching and learning of AI in schools. DT Hub is a website funded by the Australian government to support the implementation of the national Digital Technologies curriculum.

We are very proud and happy to share our story.
