9 IST 2020

9 IST girls started off with Digital Media and then moved on to web design and development. Working in teams, the girls collaboratively decided on their topic, Criteria for Success, and Style Guides. They also had to consider web accessibility guidelines (#a11y).

10 IST 2020

10 IST girls started off with case studies on Artificial Intelligence and then moved on to Data Science. Working in pairs, the girls explored data processes of collection/extraction, analysis, and visualisation. The Big Data-Dear Data project allowed them to compare and contrast working with bigger datasets and personal data.
